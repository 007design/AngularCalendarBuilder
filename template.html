Step 1
Browse through the available photos by selecting a season from the drop-down menu.
Step 2
Choose your photos by clicking on a month in your Current Order (to the right)
and then clicking on a photo for that month.
Step 3
When you have finished choosing photos, click on the Submit button at the bottom of the page.
Step 4
After viewing your proof, click the Back button at the bottom of the Proof page
if you would like to change an image.
